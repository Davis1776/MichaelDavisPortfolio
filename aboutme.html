About Me

A closer look at my professional experience, education, and career transition

Professional Experience

With a proven history of spearheading innovative programs, I have taken a data analytics and research driven approach to managing projects across the country while leading marketing, advocacy, and digital campaigns. Working in over 30 states, I have employed a collaborative research-based approach to restructuring and growing powerful organizations while generating over $33 million in fundraising.

In 2007, I opened my own consulting firm which focused on developing targeted messaging for business organizations. I have served as President and CEO of multiple statewide associations, SVP at a national association, and as a Vice President for the Indiana Chamber of Commerce. I have had the opportunity to give hundreds of presentations to CEOs, business leaders, clients, board of directors, media, and other statekholders.

While leading a statewide business association in Washington state, I led the developement of a statwide data platform that created models of audiences to microtarget persuadable groups by collecting over 4,000 data points on nearly seven million individuals. In 2010, I created and led the team that developed a strategic and cutting-edge digital campaign which included the first-ever U.S. nationwide buyout of YouTube by a political candidate or organization that was described in the media as one of the five “best of the best programs” across the country. In 2012, I again created and led a team that was among the first three clients in the nation to utilize Twitter's promoted products for the three Presidential debates.

Career Transition

My strongest passion is in data analysis and using predictive analytics to deliver a product or message to a specific audience. I am a well organized, detail oriented individual with strong interpersonal and presentation skills that loves to use a data-driven, research-based aproach to tell a story from an interesting dataset.

I am currently making a career transition and would like to help a company by using my enhanced data analytical skills to work closely with internal technical teams and outward facing clients/customers/constituents. I am looking for a role as either a business intelligence analyst, data analyst, or technical project director. I believe my previous roles where I served as an executive with several organizations paired with my new data skills will provide value to a company by connecting seasoned leadership and soft skills with the technical aspects of data analytics. This is an exciting transitional period and I am looking forward to the future!

Education and Continued Learning

I have also owned a successful consulting firm specializing in public opinion research, business analytics, demographic analysis, digital marketing, and organizational management while representing Fortune 500 companies, national and state associations, local government, and media organizations. I believe strongly in continuing education and completed the Butler University Data Analytics and Visualizaion Executive Education certificate program in May, 2020. This bootcamp covered an extensive curriculum of Python, SQL, Tableau, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Machine Learning, Tableau, and Excel.

I have continued to enjoy expanding my skills and have recently earned additional certificates from the University of California, Davis in Essential Design Principles for Tableau and Fundamentals of Visualization with Tableau.

In 2018, I earned a Customer Analytics Certificate from The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. Previously, I earned the Institute of Organizational Management (IOM) professional designation from the U.S. Chamber of Commerce and I received my BA in Political Science with a minor in Economics from Indiana University.
