Tableau Data Visualizations

Visualizing Honey Bee Data with Tableau

Background

In this project, I built a Tableau Story consisting of nine dashboards displaying the changes in honey production (lbs.), honey value ($), and almond acres bearing. The honey bee story looks at the national and state levels with additional insight at the county level for Indiana.

For the best viewing experience, I recommend selecting the "Full Scren" option in the bottom right corner of the Tableau viz screen.



You can also view this honey bee story visualization on my Tableau Public Page
